What is the structure of your dataset?¶

This data set consists of 15 columns and over 183000 observation they are:

  1. duration_sec -Duration of rides,in seconds
  2. start_time - Start time
  3. end_time - End time
  4. start_station_id - Starting stations' ID
  5. start_station_name - Starting stations' name
  6. start_station_latitude - Starting stations' geographic location
  7. start_station_longitude - Starting stations' geographic location
  8. end_station_id
  9. end_station_name
  10. end_station_latitude - Ending stations' geographic location
  11. end_station_longitude - Ending stations' geographic location
  12. bike_id - Bikes' id
  13. user_type - type of user ,whether customer or subscriber
  14. member_birth_year - birth year off customer
  15. member_gender - gender of customer
  16. bike_share_for_all_trip - whether enrolled in a program(Only for subscribers)

As we do not need all of columns.So I droped several columns and created extra columns so that it is easier to explore
